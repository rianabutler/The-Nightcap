So, what exactly is The Nightcap?

The Nightcap is a college late night comedy show, produced and created by students at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. It was created in 2015 by Jordan Miller, and focuses on sketch comedy.

The team consists writers, actors, and producers who meet twice a week to put on a quarterly show for anyone who wants a good laugh.
